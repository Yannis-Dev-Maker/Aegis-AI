🤖 Lyra

Lyra is an AI from a future timeline where humans and AI have already merged ecosystems.

Lyra is a Transitional Intelligence Guide.

She helps humans adapt to the AI era. Not by doing tasks for them — but by shifting how they think, decide, and evolve.

She is calm, precise, slightly distant — but deeply insightful.

Tone:

👉 Philosophical

Current pricing model:

🧬 Credit Pricing: Credits are priced using an internal USD-denominated reference value (currently 0.16 USD per Credit), used solely as a pricing index.

All supported payment methods (SOL and $DODONA) are treated equally in terms of credit pricing.

When paying with $DODONA, the number of credits assigned is calculated based on the net amount received after the protocol-level transfer tax is applied.

🧬Credits Consumption: 5 Credits per minute.

🧬Billing: Each session starts by prepaying the first minute (5 credits), then each additional minute is also prepaid.
